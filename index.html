Common Questions

How many delivery batches are there?

4 daily batches: 9:30 to 11:30 AM, 11:30 AM to 1:30 PM, 4:30 to 6:30 PM, and 6:30 to 8:30 PM. Each batch is prepared fresh 1 hour before delivery.

How fresh are the salads?

Prepared exactly 1 hour before delivery. No pre-made, no frozen. Fresh like home-made, every single order.

Do you have Jain options?

Yes! Original Milk Paneer (verified) used. Root vegetables, onion, and garlic can be excluded. Select Jain option at time of purchase.

What is included in the 5-day trial?

₹1,250 for 5 fresh salad meals (one per day). Use promo code TRIAL at checkout for ₹150 off (pay only ₹1,100). No auto-renewal, no commitment. Full refund if not satisfied.

Can I pause or cancel my subscription?

Both plans have no auto-renewal. The Monthly (25-day) plan ends automatically after 25 days — re-subscribe anytime you want to continue. Pause anytime by WhatsApp at 9712989498.

Do you offer condition-specific plans?

Yes! Weight Loss, Diabetes, Thyroid, PCOD/PCOS, High Protein, and Office Tiffin plans available. WhatsApp with your health goal for customization.

Can NOSH7 help with weight loss?

Yes! Every bowl is high fibre, low calorie, and portion controlled (350–420g). High fibre keeps you full longer, reduces cravings, and supports natural fat loss. Many customers lose 2–4 kg in their first month on the 25-day plan.

How much protein is in each bowl?

Each NOSH7 bowl contains 20–25g of protein — from Original Milk Paneer, seeds, and legumes. That's complete protein in a pure veg meal, ideal for muscle retention and weight management.

Is the bowl big enough? Will I feel full?

Absolutely. Every bowl is 350–420 grams — a complete, filling meal, not a side salad. High protein + high fibre combination keeps most people satisfied for 3–4 hours. It's designed to replace a full lunch or dinner.
