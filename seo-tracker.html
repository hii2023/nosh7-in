The big picture: the website itself is excellent. On-page and technical SEO are near the ceiling, so more tweaks there move the needle very little. Competitors outrank you because of off-page factors: domain age, backlinks, the Google Map Pack and directories owning local results, inconsistent listing data, and the nosh7.in / nosh7.com authority split. Focus effort below on the Off-site & Local and Strategic sections.

Why competitors rank higher

Structural reasons, not website quality.

Domain age & backlinks

saladtime.in, tatvagreens.in and others have years of history and inbound links. nosh7.in is new (2026) with almost none. Trust takes 3 to 6+ months.

The Map Pack wins local

For "salad delivery Ahmedabad" Google shows 3 businesses on a map first. That ranking comes from your Google Business Profile, reviews and proximity, not your site.

Directories own organic

"Best tiffin/salad Ahmedabad" is JustDial, Zomato, magicpin, Wanderlog, listicles. You get listed in them rather than outrank them.

Inconsistent listing data

Zomato/magicpin show ₹999 trial / ₹199 meal while the site says ₹1,250 / ₹220. Conflicts erode local trust. Make every listing identical.

.in vs .com split

Both domains rank for "NOSH7" and compete for the same authority. Pick one, redirect the other, concentrate the links.

Thin brand & social signals

Active Instagram/FB drive brand searches and engagement that correlate with ranking. Build a consistent social presence.

How to rank for "any area of Ahmedabad"

The honest playbook for showing up when someone searches "[area] healthy food / salad / tiffin / diet meal".

  1. One landing page per area you deliver. You already have ~14 (Satellite, Bodakdev, Vastrapur, Thaltej, Sola, Bopal, Gota, Prahlad Nagar, Navrangpura, Paldi, Sarkhej, Vejalpur, Ashram Road...). Add the rest of your covered zone: Ranip, Chandlodiya, Shilaj, Naranpura, Usmanpura, Vadaj, Science City, Jodhpur, Makarba, Juhapura, Shela. Each page must be genuinely unique, not a find-and-replace clone.
  2. Make each page locally real. Name actual landmarks, societies, office parks, gyms and roads in that area. Add an area-specific FAQ and a short "delivery in [area]" paragraph. Google rewards real local relevance.
  3. LocalBusiness schema with areaServed on every area page (you do this). Keep the address/phone identical to your Google Business Profile.
  4. Set delivery areas in Google Business Profile. A service-area business can list every neighbourhood you deliver to. This is what feeds the map pack for area and "near me" searches.
  5. Target area + intent combos: "[area] weight loss meal", "[area] PCOD diet food", "[area] office lunch subscription", "[area] Jain tiffin". Lower competition, high intent.
  6. Get reviews that mention areas. Ask customers to mention their neighbourhood in Google reviews. Google uses review text for local relevance.
  7. Interlink area pages from the main Ahmedabad page and from each other ("also serving..."), and earn a few local links per area (society groups, gyms, RWAs).
  8. Reality check: the map pack favours businesses physically near the searcher, so a single kitchen ranks strongest near Satellite. Dedicated area pages win the organic results, and GBP service areas + reviews stretch your map reach. Aim to own organic everywhere and the map pack in your core radius.
