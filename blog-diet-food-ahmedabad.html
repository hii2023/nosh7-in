Why Most "Diet Food" in Ahmedabad Does Not Work

There are three common traps people in Ahmedabad fall into when trying to eat for weight loss:

  1. Crash diets and extreme restriction. Cutting calories below 1,000/day causes rapid muscle loss, slows metabolism, and leads to bingeing within 2–3 weeks.
  2. Detox juices and liquid cleanses. They feel clean but deliver almost no protein and no sustained energy. You lose water weight and gain it back immediately.
  3. Boiled/tasteless food. When food is boring, you cannot sustain it. Most people quit within 10 days and return to their old habits.

Sustainable weight loss requires a caloric deficit of 300–500 calories/day, high protein (to preserve muscle), adequate fibre (to stay full), and food you can actually enjoy eating every day.
